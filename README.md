# 🕹️ PINBALL WIZARD 26 RGB (Next-Gen VR/Universal Controller)

[![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Work--in--Progress-orange.svg)](https://github.com/VR-addicted/PINBALL-WIZARD-26-GAMEPAD)
[![Hardware](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-ESP32--P4%20(RISC--V)-blue.svg)](https://github.com/VR-addicted/PINBALL-WIZARD-26-GAMEPAD)

**The world’s first telescopic, ergonomically adaptive, and quad-powered VR/HID Pinball Controller.**

The **Pinball Wizard 26** is the ultimate evolution of the PW-25. It bridges the gap between physical arcade haptics and virtual reality immersion. Whether you are playing *Pinball FX* on a Meta Quest or pro-level arcade sims on PC, the PW-26 is built to outperform.

---

## 🚀 Key Innovations

### 🔋 Quad-Cell "Quest-Charge" System
Unlike dual-cell setups, the PW-26 features **four 18650 Li-Ion batteries** driving an onboard **Power Bank Controller**. 
* **Infinite Play:** Keep your VR headset charged while you play.
* **Dedicated Display:** Real-time monitoring of your power reserves.

### 📏 Telescopic "Width-Adjust" Chassis
The frame expands from **50 cm to 64 cm**.
* **Total Immersion:** Align the controller's physical width to the virtual table's scale in VR.
* **Versatile Placement:** Use it on your desk (slid over your keyboard) or wedge it between your legs while sitting on the couch for a rock-solid, stable base.

### 🔄 Rotatable Button Pods (Innovation)
Featuring our custom **Rotor System** with two buttons per side.
* **Adjustable Ergonomics:** Rotate the buttons to match your natural hand position.
* **Pro-Grade Hardware:** Built for **Qanba Gravity RGB** buttons for maximum tactile feedback and durability.

### 🌐 Universal HID Beast
While we love the Meta Quest, the PW-26 is a universal weapon:
* **Bluetooth HID** for wireless VR and mobile gaming.
* **Wired USB-C** for low-latency desktop/PC gaming.
* **RISC-V Power:** Driven by the **ESP32-P4**, allowing for high-resolution 4.3" displays and complex RGB mapping.

---

## 🛠️ Features at a Glance
* **4.3" Central LCD + 7-Segment Score Display:** Retro aesthetics meet modern tech.
* **Wireless Foot Pedal:** Wireless trigger for "Action Mode" (wireless foot control).
* **Nudge Support:** Use physical movement to influence the ball, just like a real machine.
* **RGB Underglow:** Dynamic lighting that reacts to the game state.
* **ABS Heavy-Duty Construction:** Engineered for stability and durability.
